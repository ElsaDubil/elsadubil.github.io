Performing Arts

My Background

I began my acrobatics education in my hometown of Salt Lake City, Utah, joining the local performing arts company "Aerobatics" during high school.

Studying with Aeris Aerial Arts Company, I received my formal education in aerial silks and straps before moving to Los Angeles for university.

Once in LA, I formed the nonprofit organization Cirque de 'LA, with the mission of providing free and low-cost aerial arts education to UCLA students and the greater Los Angeles area.

As founder of Cirque de 'LA, I have taught over 300 students and performed for over 7,000 people. If you wish to join our organization, contact me with the form at the bottom of the page and one of our board members will get in contact with you.
